Now ask people you physically meet with to scan your QR code



How this works:

Your email address is included in the QR code.

When someone scans your QR code it opens their email app, with a pre-filled email addressed to you.

They simply click send, sending an email to you.

Now you both have each other's email address and a record of the time you met.

Later on, you can search your emails for "Handshake Record" as the subject in both sent and received emails.

This information can be used to help with contact tracing.
